FAQ

Common questions.

What file types can I send? +
PDFs, Word documents, PowerPoints, and plain text. Anything text-based can be converted.
How long does initial setup take? +
After an onboarding call, one-time creative setup takes around 2 days. Every video after that is turned around in 24 hours.
What does the £12k subscription include? +
60 minutes of finished video per year, creative setup, dedicated account manager, 40+ language support, and completion tracking. Bespoke elements are priced per hour.
Can we add custom branded elements? +
Yes. As a fully managed service we add custom animations, icons, character interactions, and on-screen text — from a simple logo overlay to a full animated sequence.
Which languages are supported? +
40+ languages. Request the same video in multiple languages simultaneously — ideal for global compliance or international training rollouts.

Yes. We offer reseller licences for agencies and L&D consultancies. Get in touch or visit the reseller section above to learn more.
